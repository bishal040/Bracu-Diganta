### Outreach

---

<!-- Page 10 Content -->

The Diganta Outreach Program is a nationwide initiative by

BRACU Diganta aimed at expanding access to space and

STEM education beyond university campuses. Launched as a

student-led national outreach effort, the program developed

structured curricula, hands-on learning kits, and workshop

modules to promote experiential learning. Through

interactive outreach workshops featuring live engineering

demonstrations, students across multiple institutions were

introduced to CanSat components, sensors, and system-level

design concepts. By emphasizing problem-solving,

mentorship, and STEM career guidance, the initiative

engaged and inspired future engineers and researchers

while continuously refining its impact through participant

feedback.Bringing Space Education

Beyond the Classroom

### Raihana Shams Islam Antara

Raihana Shams Islam Antara is a Lecturer in EEE at

the BSRM School of Engineering, BRAC University.

She was a core engineer of BRAC Onnesha and co-

founded LASSET. She serves as the Bangladesh Point

of Contact for UNISEC-Global and has contributed

internationally through NASA Space Apps Challenge

judging and the BIRDS International Workshop.

Her achievements include the BRACU Research Seed

Grant (2022, Co-PI), ACI Youth Excellence Award,

contribution to the 2017 GEDC Airbus Diversity Award,

Best Paper Award at Kyushu Institute of Technology,

leadership roles in Women in Big Data and IEEE

ComSoc BRACU, and being the first Bangladeshi

graduate of the CanSat Leadership Programme.Abdulla Hil Kafi
